Welcome to Cylindric3D's MakerFarm Parts

X-Carriage Side Bracket

This replaces the small triangular side-bracket of the X-Carriage. The OpenSCAD file has parameters for cutting a hole to fit a servo, or the normal end-stop bolt. There are also (optional) holes at the lower end of the bracket to attach other accessories, such as LED rings, probe-mounts, whatever.

RaspberryPi Camera Bracket

A simple bracket to mount a RaspberryPi camera module to the back of the printer. The OpenSCAD file has parameters in it to allow adjusting how far across the camera will sit.
